Leave Management System

Smart online leave management system that allows users to apply for their leave conveniently anytime, anywhere.

LMS is a web-based Leave Management System that streamlines communication between HR and employees and facilitates simple yet efficient management of employee leave. For employees, this system allows them to easily determine their vacation availability (from any point-in-time) and request leave online. For managers, leave approval no longer involves trails of paperwork. This ensures that all leaves taken and leave requests are properly monitored, where everybody can apply and view leave records without the hassle and delay of going through the admin staff - it's all at your fingertips with anytime, anywhere access.
